⚖️ Honest Assessment: Where Vanilla Has Value

The vanilla prompt is not useless — it provides fast, good-enough coverage for the most obvious vulnerabilities. Understanding where it adds value helps organizations choose the right approach.

🎯 Key Takeaways

🎯

Systematic Coverage

The pen tester checks all 60 controls every time. Vanilla prompts cover what catches its attention — no guarantee of completeness.

🧠

CIA + Compliance Context

Every finding is anchored to security impact (CIA) and regulatory requirements (OWASP, NIST, ISO). Vanilla gives findings without business context.

🤖

AI Skill Security Gap

Vanilla has zero framework for assessing Claude skill security. Prompt injection (SKILL-002, SKILL-005) and scope creep (SKILL-007) require a dedicated skill control library to detect.
